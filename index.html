Let Me Sleep Mobile Application

Are you ready to get more zzz's?

Summary

Let Me Sleep gives users an idea of how productive they will be based on their quality of sleep. Users will also have the opportunity to improve their sleeping habits by taking steps in developing a 'pro-sleep' routine and by creating a strong connection between their bed and sleep.

Target Audience

Young adults who have difficulty getting a good nightâ€™s rest. Other audiences include anyone looking into improving their sleeping habits and people interested in learning more about how sleep affects their cognitive functions.

Market Place

There are many mobile apps that track your sleep patterns but there isn't a single app that informs users how their quality of sleep affects their cognitive functions.

Process

From Research to Implementation

1. Gathering Research

I've been struggling with sleep for as long as I can remember. I never took the quality of my sleep serious until it started affecting my heatlh. I was having a difficult time being productive throughout the day. I was constantly tired, I kept forgetting details in assignments. It was difficult for me to pay attention in class, overall, I knew I wasn't performing my best. During winter break, I was trying to find apps that could help me understand my quality of sleep but I couldn't find any. That's when I realized I needed to solve this problem, not only for myself but for others.

I started my process by developing a plan to execute this idea and stay on task with my deliverables.

I conducted two months worth of research to better understand sleep and how it affects the cognitive functions of the brain. I recorded my sleeping patterns in a sleep log and even participated in a sleep study to better understand the target users. Lastly, I interviewed four sleep doctors which helped me narrow down the features I wanted to include in my app.
